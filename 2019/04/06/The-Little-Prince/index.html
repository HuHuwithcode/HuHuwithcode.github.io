The Little Prince

Posted on 2019-04-06 | Edited on 2019-04-14 | In Fun Project | Comments: | Views:

[February 2018 - June 2018]
Course: Introduction to Web Design

The Little Prince is an interactive story website for children aged 13 and under. The project aims to design a digital version of a printed “choose users own adventure” book. The entire website was designed through three iterations process to explore how it can most effectively translate the book from physical to digital form.

Introduction

The website is basing on a household French story- Little Prince. A poetic tale with watercolour illustrations, this story contains several fantastic adventure experience of the little prince. The little prince, the pilot and the narrator are brought to a desert due to a crashed aircraft, which makes a beginning of the story.

Project Description

Interesting Aspect

This story is basically a reflection on Saint-Exupéry’s own experience in the Sahara in a fairy tale writing style. What makes the story well-known and extraordinary is the critical social aspect. This story reflects human nature instead of a simple fiction adventure, describing an absurd adult world. People in various ages are inclined to make different understanding and reflection on this story. The six people that the prince meets are meant to critique an appearance of society. The king without subjects desire for followers, which is quite similarly with some politicians who are not capable of management talent but to pursue power in their life. Besides, a businessman who ignores the beauty of stars and keeps counting them with a perverted value in this story is same as some people who desire for wealth and material possessions without interest in spiritual matters. These people are all typical symbols in reality.

The Audience

Commonly, people consider The Little Prince as a children’s book. In the matter of fact, many adults continue to cling to this story long after childhood, which means that the audience is of all ages( ，2005). Young children would find it an interesting and imaginative story while adults tend to understand this story in different perspectives, combining their life experiences. Everyone could find something in common with the six strange people. The process of grown-ups equals to the time of understanding this story. This story is especially suitable for every child and some adults who lost their innocence and thinking ability. The prince cares for colours of a small house, a common but beautiful flower and the relationship between others, which often ignored by grown-ups. People are supposed to care about each other, cherish what’s really important for them, and be grateful for the happiness that they’ve gained. We all believe knowledge and experience enable us to see clearly and make right judgements all the time. But we may sometimes lose the right to speak straight forwardly, think creatively or love courageously resulting from what we’ve experienced. We all once have those doubts the little prince has, and the story reminds us of these doubts when we grow up. This website is intention to display this story again for touching the soul and arousing thinking. Only to hope that people can be aware of the simplicity of life. ( Wikipedia,2017)

Conclusion

With the development of society, people are inclined to be a ‘grown-up’, ignoring the beauty and simplicity. We are supposed to care about each other, cherish what’s really important for them. This website based on the story the Little Prince intends to display this story again for touching the soul and arousing thinking. Only to hope that people can be aware of the simplicity of life.
