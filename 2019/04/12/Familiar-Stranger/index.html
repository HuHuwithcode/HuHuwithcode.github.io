Familiar Stranger

Posted on 2019-04-12 | Edited on 2019-04-14 | In summary | Comments: | Views:

[July 2018 - November 2018]
Course Name: Social & Mobile Computing

“Familiar Stranger” is a design concept which try to evoke people’s self-awareness of mobile addiction. It includes a IOS mobile app prototype and a smart case which made by RFduino.

Introduction

Because of the development of mobile technology, People’s lives are closely connected to mobile phones. At the same time, the trend of young people and teenagers gradually become the main force of mobile addiction. These people are passionate about communicating with friends and sharing their lives on the social media of mobile Phones. It is precisely for this reason that they are compressed in real life with people’s Real communication time, and have less chance to communicate with their peers. After an investigation, most students seldom or never try to take the initiative to get in-depth communication with classmates in the same classroom. Even if they all recognise each other, they are also the most familiar Strangers. Our team studied domain focus on social media addiction, and delve into and analyse the causes and effects of this Phenomenon. At the same time, our team was committed to developing a system or equipment to assist in increasing people’s face-to-face communication opportunities.

Project Description

Prototype Overview

As we talked with Tutor and researched before, social textile as an inspiration for us to develop a design opportunity named “familiar stranger”. Our purpose is bringing people to build a new relationship and maintain their friendship in the real world instead of over-relying on social media application. Many people that they meet same people every day in the same place like in the school, the bus station but they never talked with each other or they lack the opportunity to chat with others. Our concept provides a chance for them to communicate with those people and enable them not addicted to social media.

In order to make our concept more attractive to inhabitants, our team member did a questionnaire to ascertain the form of our design, which is phone case. Hence, we linked our idea with the phone case that showed the above pictures. Candidates who have the same phone case they could adjust their current status with emoji such as happy to talk, sad today, really do not want to talk to anyone and etc, which they change the status and people could see their current status to choose whether or not chat with someone.

Scenario

Physical Prototype

The initial hypothesis of our group is to create a prototype of the mobile phone case that simulates the following figure, which consists of two parts, one is to display the light function of different colours, and the other is the display which can display different expression packs. The phone case which can display the users’ emotion, green means they are willing to chat with others, red means do not want to be disturbed. The user can change their emotion by click on the small button on the side of the phone case. People who see other’s phone case display the green light can talk with them without hesitation, while for those who make their phone case display red light can avoid some embarrassing situations.Thus, I build the physical prototype by using the RFduino and sensoring design.

The entire device was divided into two parts, one is an internal structure that contains the transformed lights controlled by RFdunio. One was an external structure, mainly to simulate the production of a mobile phone case.

This is the screen that I have intended to display the emojis that user choose. Due to the time limitation, I did not achieve our goal, but I will research and try to implement this function in the future.
